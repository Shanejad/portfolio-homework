ABOUT ME

My combined experiences in different areas of design from architectural design to UX/UI design has taught me a lot about design thinking process and most importantly user behavior. I learnt to have sympathy for my user. Working with teams in both educational and professional settings taught me how to communicate my personal ideas and shape them for the best result to be accomplished by the team. There is always so much growth and fun in working with a group and I truly enjoy that. On the other hand, being so passionate about languages, I started learning HTML and CSS through Codeacademy and leveled up my technical knowledge while studying HTML,CSS and Javascript in UCLA bootcamp. I believe I can perform a great role in writing, iterating and using code which gives me the opportunity to work on a broader range of interesting projects. My skills include user experience design, UI design, Web design, Wire framing, low to high fidelity prototyping, user research, usability testing, heuristic research, responsive design and coding it, team playing, constantly communicating my code with my collaborators through Github and GitKraken and being updated about their iterations.
